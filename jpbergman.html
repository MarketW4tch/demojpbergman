J.P. Bergman was established on the premise that most Australians receive mortgage advice shaped by the broker's commercial interests rather than their own. This firm operates differently.


With access to a panel of more than forty lenders — spanning major banks, mutual institutions, and non-bank specialists — every engagement begins with your balance sheet, your objectives, and your timeline. Not a product shelf.

How We Work

A structured approach
from first call to settlement.

01

Discovery & Assessment

An initial consultation to understand your financial position, objectives, and timeframe in full. No credit enquiries at this stage. This conversation determines strategy before any lender is approached.

02

Lender Selection & Structuring

Your file is assessed against the lending criteria of suitable institutions. Rate is only one variable — serviceability treatment, policy flexibility, and approval timeframes are weighted equally.

03

Application & Negotiation

Documentation collated, application submitted, and rate negotiation conducted on your behalf. Direct lender relationships allow access to pricing not available through standard lodgement channels.

04

Settlement & Ongoing Review

Full coordination through to settlement. Post-settlement, your loan is monitored annually against current market conditions. Rate complacency costs most borrowers — this practice prevents it.

More than forty lenders on panel, including specialist non-bank institutions that offer structures the major banks will not consider. Your options are not limited by a restricted panel.
